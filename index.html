About Katherine

Katherine Low Kai Xin is a Junior Partner at Tan Wei Ceat & Co (TWC Law), a full-service legal firm with offices in Kuala Lumpur, Penang, Johor Bahru and Kedah. Holding an LLB (Hons) and having passed the Certificate in Legal Practice (CLP), she is an Advocate & Solicitor of the High Court of Malaya.

She advises individuals, families and businesses with a focus on being approachable, responsive and clear — translating legal complexity into decisions clients can act on with confidence. Whether you are buying a home, structuring a business, or resolving a dispute, Katherine is committed to protecting your interests every step of the way.
